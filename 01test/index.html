What is Digital Citizenship?

Digital citizenship means using the internet safely, responsibly, and respectfully. It's about knowing how to protect yourself online, treat others well, and make smart choices when using technology. This idea shows up in schools, workplaces, and everyday life because nearly everything we do now involves digital tools

What is Digital Law?

h2>Digital law refers to the rules, rights, and responsibilities that govern behavior in the online world.

What is AUP?

An Acceptable Use Policy outlines what users can and cannot do when using a network or digital service.

What is Plagiarism?

Plagiarism is the act of taking someone else's words, ideas, or work and presenting them as your own without giving proper credit.

What is Academic Dishonesty?

Academic dishonesty refers to any behavior in a school or university setting where a student gains an unfair academic advantage by breaking rules of honesty and integrity.
